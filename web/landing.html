Tally

Tally pulls your BC Self-Serve data and D2L school grades into one clean dashboard. Benefits, payments, course progress -- no portal maze required. Sign in to get started.

What you get

  • Payments at a glance -- See your next payment date and amount instantly. No more logging in and clicking through five screens to find a number.
  • Messages & notifications -- All your BC Self-Serve messages and notifications in one clean list. Read them without fighting the government portal UI.
  • School grades -- D2L Brightspace grade scraper, learning guide auto-fill, and dropbox submission. See your course progress without the D2L maze.
  • Your credentials, secure -- Your logins are encrypted end-to-end. Tally never stores passwords in plain text or shares your data.

How it works

  • Sign in with your BCEID -- Enter the same username and password you use on BC Self-Serve. Tally handles the login on your behalf.
  • Tally fetches your data -- We pull your payments, messages, and notifications automatically. Fresh data every time you check in.
  • Read it in plain English -- No jargon, no government-speak. Just the information you need, laid out clearly.

Free to use. No account needed beyond your existing logins. Check your benefits now.
