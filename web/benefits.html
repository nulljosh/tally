‚ùå CDB (Canada Disability Benefit) - Not Eligible Yet

Why blocked: You need DTC approval FIRST before you can apply for CDB.

After DTC Gets Approved:

  1. Service Canada sends you invitation letter to apply for CDB
  2. You apply for CDB online at canada.ca/cdb
  3. If approved, you get back pay from approval month
  4. Monthly $200 payments start (BC won't claw this back)

Final monthly income: $1,600 (PWD) + $200 (CDB) = $1,800/month

Key Rules

  • Save up to $100,000 without losing disability benefits
  • CDB $200/month won't reduce your disability benefits
  • Must have DTC to qualify for CDB
  • Always report income/asset changes to Ministry
